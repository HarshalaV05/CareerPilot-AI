# CareerPilot AI

> An Enterprise Agentic Career Counseling & Placement Intelligence Platform powered by IBM watsonx.ai, IBM Granite, and watsonx Orchestrate.

## 📌 IBM SkillsBuild AICTE Internship 2026

### Problem Statement
Agentic Career Counseling Companion

### Project Objective

CareerPilot AI is an intelligent career guidance platform that leverages IBM Granite and watsonx Orchestrate to provide personalized career recommendations, skill-gap analysis, learning roadmaps, internship suggestions, interview preparation, and placement readiness insights.
